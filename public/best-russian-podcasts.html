🎧 Audio

Best Free Russian Podcasts

Listening trains your ear faster than any textbook. These curated Russian podcasts span every CEFR level — from slow-spoken stories for absolute beginners to native-speed conversations for C1+.

Why podcasts work

Podcasts give you authentic Russian prosody, intonation, and connected speech — things textbooks can never teach. Even passive listening (while commuting or cooking) trains your brain to segment Russian into words.

How to pick the right one

  • A1–A2: Look for "slow Russian" formats with transcripts.
  • B1–B2: Story-driven podcasts and learner-targeted news.
  • C1–C2: Native podcasts on culture, politics, comedy, or your hobbies.
