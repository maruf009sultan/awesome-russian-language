Free Russian Cyrillic alphabet quiz online

The Russian alphabet — Cyrillic — has 33 letters: 10 vowels, 21 consonants, and 2 signs (the hard sign Ъ and the soft sign Ь). This free interactive quiz randomly tests you on the sound of each letter, including tricky ones like Ы, Щ, Х, Ж, and Ц. Perfect for beginners at the A1 CEFR level who are starting their Russian language journey. Practice your Cyrillic alphabet, learn Russian letter sounds, and prepare for reading real Russian text.
